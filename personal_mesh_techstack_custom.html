2

Turn WAV → MFCC features → feed into a tiny PyTorch classifier. Start with just 5–10 words.

Start with a simple RNN or tiny Transformer. Train on your own text data. Get it to complete sentences.
4
Week 4

Save your conversations → AI starts remembering. Combine with your text model for smart replies.
5
Week 5
Wrap it in Streamlit
Connect everything — voice input, your LLM, memory — into one chat interface. Your Personal Mesh is alive! 🎉
